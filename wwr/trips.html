Pick your raft size.

There are 4 different sizes of rafts to choose from. This allows us to pair you with another group or accomodate a larger group. Everyone will work together to get the raft down the river. We can accomodate all groups!

Choose your difficulty level.

You can choose between calm waters that is ideal for fishing to great white rapids that will give the greatest thrills. Timing is everything as you plan your next trip. June is great for large rapids and higher water level. July is calmer but still has plenty of rapids. August is great for fishing and a more calm adventure.

When to sign up.

Trips are ran weekly starting the first week of June. You can choose between 3-day, 4-day, and 5-day adventures. If you are planning on bringing your kids, don't forget to sign-up for our 10th annual kids trip starting the middle of July.
