Why Rapids?

Rapids has over 20 years experience providing fun for all ages. They know the best places to raft and can make recommendations to you and/or your party to make sure you enjoy your trip. Trust us to show you an exciting time!

The Ride

Utah’s Long Fork of the Trout rips down 50 miles of road-less, basically untouched land. Its reputation for white water rafting is not exaggerated. Depending on the time of year, you can expect constant big bumps or more smooth white water rapids that never fail to thrill. June is whitewater perfection, July is great for all abilities and August offers great rapids and really great fishing.
