    Utah

    Utah is a state that contains the Great Salt Lake and parts of the Rocky Mountains and Uinta Mountains. The western part of the state is desertlike. About 70% of the land is owned by the federal or state government. There are many outdoor activities to enjoy in Utah, including: skiing, hiking in its multiple national parks, biking, and rock climbing. Utah experiences all 4 seasons and should be visited in each of them to get a true feel for the area.
